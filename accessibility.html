Warblr — Accessibility

Warblr is built to work for everyone. It supports:

  • VoiceOver — every control is labeled; results are grouped into clear, spoken summaries (species and likelihood); decorative elements are hidden; saved birds expose a “Share” action; saving a bird is announced.
  • Dynamic Type — text and the main controls scale with your preferred text size, and the result screen scrolls so nothing is clipped.
  • Reduce Motion — the ambient flying-birds animation and the listening pulse stop when Reduce Motion is on.
  • Reduce Transparency — translucent surfaces become solid.
  • Color & contrast — meaning is never carried by color alone; text on colored chips meets WCAG AA contrast.
