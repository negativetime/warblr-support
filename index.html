Warblr — Support

Warblr identifies birds by their song or call, entirely on your device. Questions or a bug? Email negativetime@me.com.

FAQ

It didn’t recognize the bird.

Get closer, reduce wind/background noise, and try again — “Try Longer” listens for 30 seconds. Turning on “Birds near you” narrows the candidates and improves accuracy a lot.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Identification is fully on-device. The internet is only used to load a reference photo, look up a place name, or share — never to identify a bird.

Why does it ask for location?

It’s optional and approximate. It’s used on your device to limit results to species that are actually near you at this time of year. It is never sent anywhere.

It heard more than one bird.

If two or more species are clearly present, Warblr tells you and lists each. If it’s unsure between similar birds, it shows the top contenders with a likelihood split.

Where are my saved birds?

In the Found Birds tab — each with its photo, approximate location, and date. They’re stored only on your device. Swipe or long-press a bird to share a field card.

Accessibility

Warblr supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion, Reduce Transparency, and high contrast. See the accessibility statement for details.
